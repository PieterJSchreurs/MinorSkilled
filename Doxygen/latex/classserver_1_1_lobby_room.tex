\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classserver_1_1_lobby_room}{Lobby\+Room}} is a little bit more extensive than the \mbox{\hyperlink{classserver_1_1_login_room}{Login\+Room}}. In this room clients change their \textquotesingle{}ready status\textquotesingle{}. If enough people are ready, they are automatically moved to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classserver_1_1_game_room}{Game\+Room}} to play a Game (assuming a game is not already in play).
