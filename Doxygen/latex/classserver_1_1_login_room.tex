\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classserver_1_1_login_room}{Login\+Room}} is the first room clients \textquotesingle{}enter\textquotesingle{} until the client identifies himself with a Player\+Join\+Request. If the client sends the wrong type of request, it will be kicked.

A connected client that never sends anything will be stuck in here for life, unless the client disconnects (that will be detected in due time).
